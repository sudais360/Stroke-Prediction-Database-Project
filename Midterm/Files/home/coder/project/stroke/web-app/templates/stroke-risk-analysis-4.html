Health Parameters Analysis for Stroke Patients

This analysis presents key observations on the mean and standard deviation for various health parameters among stroke patients:

  • Systolic Blood Pressure: Mean - 135.05 mm Hg, Std Dev - 26.37.
  • Diastolic Blood Pressure: Mean - 84.90 mm Hg, Std Dev - 14.68.
  • HDL Cholesterol Level: Mean - 55.06 mg/dL, Std Dev - 14.84.
  • LDL Cholesterol Level: Mean - 125.05 mg/dL, Std Dev - 37.59.
  • Average Glucose Level: Mean - 130.08 mg/dL, Std Dev - 40.69.

It's important to note that these statistical measures do not imply a correlation or causation between the health parameters and stroke incidence. Correlation analysis requires more complex statistical methods, and causation can only be established through controlled experimental designs or longitudinal studies. Further research with appropriate statistical methods is needed to explore potential correlations.
